The Foot Book

By:  Dr. Suess

This is my favorite book of all time.

About the book

This book is a childrens book that was written by Dr. Suess and published in 1969 by Random House. This book is all about feet. (not in a weird way) The book only has 132 words in it and 47 of those words are some form of the word 'foot.'

Other Dr. Suess Books

"The Foot Book" wasn't the only book written by Dr. Suess. There are a ton. Here are some of the more notable ones.
